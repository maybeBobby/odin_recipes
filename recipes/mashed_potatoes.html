Mashed Potatoes

Description

Mashed potatoes are awesome and easy to make. Few ingredients are needed for this recipe.

    Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Water
  • Milk

    Steps

  • cut potatoes
  • boil water
  • mash potatoes
  • add butter
  • add milk
  • add salt, pepper, garlic
  • serve
