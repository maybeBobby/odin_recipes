Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken curry is a flavorful and easy to cook dish. It is also a very healthy dish. There are many different flavors to choose for the sauce so be sure to find one that suits you!

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • curry sauce
  • rice
  • salt
  • pepper
  • vegetables
  • other spices

Steps

  • cut chicken into smaller chunks
  • season chichen with salt, pepper, and other spices
  • cover chicken with yogurt
  • cook rice in rice cooker
  • cook chicken
  • pour in curry sauce
  • serve
